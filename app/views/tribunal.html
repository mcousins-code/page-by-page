Going to an employment tribunal

What is going to an employment tribunal like?

Going to an employment tribunal:

  • takes a long time - you could wait up to 1 year for your final hearing
  • is a public process - anyone can attend the hearing
  • has a limited number of outcomes
  • does not guarantee you will win

For an employment tribunal hearing you will have to:

  • prepare all the paperwork
  • keep to the judge’s strict timetable and deadlines
  • stand up and give evidence

How to make a tribunal claim

You still need an Acas certificate to make a claim to an employment tribunal. You will use this certificate when you complete an employment tribunal claim form, called an ET1. You can find this form on the government website.

You will need to complete our notification form to request a certificate.

The form takes about 5 minutes to complete.

You will need:

  • your phone number and email address
  • your employer’s full legal name and address - see your payslip, contract, P60 or P45 for this
  • details about what’s happened
