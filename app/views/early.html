Try early conciliation

What is early conciliation?

This is when one of our conciliators speaks with you and your employer to help you come to an agreement.

The aim of this is to help you find a solution to the problem without having to go to an employment tribunal.

If you reach an agreement, your conciliator can put this into a legally binding document.

Why is it good to try early conciliation?

Our early conciliation service:

  • can settle your problem quickly
  • lasts a maximum of 6 weeks
  • is less stressful than going to an employment tribunal
  • means a conciliator speaks to your employer so you do not have to
  • gives you more options for resolving the problem
  • is easy to arrange around your day
  • is entirely confidential
  • can keep a good relationship with your employer

If you're worried about running out of time

When you tell us that you want early conciliation, the time limit for making your claim gets extended by up to 6 weeks.

This is so there's time to try and resolve the issue without you needing to go to an employment tribunal.

How to start early conciliation

You will need to complete our early conciliation notification form.

The form takes about 5 minutes to complete.

You will need:

  • your phone number and email address
  • your employer’s full legal name and address - see your payslip, contract, P60 or P45 for this
  • the contact details of the best person for the conciliator to speak to at your work
  • details about what’s happened
